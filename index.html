Special Relativity

  • The Laws of nature are the same for everyone
  • The Speed of light is the same for everyone
  • Motion is relative
\gamma = \frac{1}{\sqrt{1-v^2/c^2}}

Time Dilation

Time passes slower in a moving reference frame.

\Delta{t_{moving}} = \frac{\Delta{t_{rest}}}{\sqrt{1-v^2/c^2}}

Length Contraction

Moving objects appear shorter for the same reason that moving clocks run slow

L = L_0\sqrt{1-v^2/c^2} = \frac{L_0}{\gamma}

Science Fiction Examples

Ender's Game

In Ender's Game, Mazer Rackham is a veteran from the first invasion and is kept alive through Special Relativity so that he could assist humanity during the second invasion.

  • Mazer Rackam only ages 8 years but was away for 50 years.

Star Wars

Many people get mad when they hear Han Solo bragging about completing the Kessel Run in 12 parsecs since parsec is a unit of time but due to length contraction, this is actually something to brag about.

  • Distance Moving: 12 Parsecs
  • Distance Rest: 16 Parsecs
